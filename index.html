    Home

    Welcome to the EMScyclopedia, a website dedicated to the study of electromagnetism. This website is designed to be a resource for students and teachers alike. It contains a searchable database of electromagnetism related topics, as well as a glossary of terms. The website also contains a section for teachers, which contains lesson plans and other resources.
